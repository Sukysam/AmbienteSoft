About Us

About Us

AmbienteSoft is a creative design Company catering to the growing demand for home and office furniture, interior design concepts, soft furniture and other products.

Visit our 250sqm showroom and office in the heart of Nigeria's capital - Abuja! where you will find beautiful furniture and decorations for your new or renovated house, your office or other space you desire to 'recreate' and beautify. We are the right place for hand crafted chairs, beds, shelves, dining sets,custom made curtains, bedroom dressing and much much more.

We also have a range of products made in our Abuja factory by well trained personnel. AmbienteSoft caters to all tastes, from the clients who prefer more classic styles to the young who seek sleek European interiors. To experience the alluring ambience of the AmbienteSoft showroom, we invite you to visit us in Wuse II.
